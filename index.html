Who is Jetmax?

Over a decade, Jetmax has built a strong and reliable reputation with customers and technology providers.
In 2015, Flight Centre Travel Group acquired a 70 percent stake in the company, followed only several months later by a full acquisition.
Jetmax now outperforms many flagship Flight Centre divisions in performance, profitability, and reliability but still maintains a startup spirit and long-term goals to innovate in the travel technology space.
